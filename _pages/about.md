Myself Samrat Mukherjee. I am a first-year PhD student at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay under the supervision of Prof. Biplab Banerjee, Prof. Subhashis Chaudhuri and Prof. Gemma Roig (Goethe University, Frankfurt). 

I am working on different Computer Vision problems, in an attempt to develop more adaptive AI systems in the visual world.
I am grateful to have my PhD studies funded by the Prime Minister Research Fellowship.


Prior to joining IIT Bombay, I did my Master's degree at the Indian Institute of Technology, Patna. I was fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Asif Ekbal. During my Master's, I also interned at TCS Research, Bengaluru under Dr. Hrishikesh Sharma.
